Leadership Team

President

Vinay Rao

Vinay is a senior majoring in mechanical engineering and serves as the club's president. His primary goal is to make the club a highly valuable professional and social experience for all members, equipping them with skills and knowledge they can apply in the STEM industry. Vinay is passionate about meeting like-minded individuals who share his love for engineering and enjoys collaborating on projects that inspire creativity and innovation.
